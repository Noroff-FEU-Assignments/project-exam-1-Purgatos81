About me

I am a Norwegian photographer born In 1972. The last 25 years I have been covering events in Norway and around the world, mostly for the Norwegian national newspaper Dagbladet. My main focus has been conflict and war photography. It all started with the aftermath of the genocide in Rwanda in 1995. Since then my work have taken me to the conflicts in the Balkans, The middle east, Israel and Palestine. I have had numerous trips to Gaza. I spent several month in Afghanistan during the war in 2001. I was in Baghdad covering the American invasion in 2003. I frequented Syria, Egypt and Libya during the Arab spring in 2011/2012. East, west and central Africa has also been an area I have visited a lot during my time as a photojournalist. I have also had the pleasure of working with stories in the far east, China, Japan, the Philippines and East Timor and several other countries. As well as Russia and the united States
